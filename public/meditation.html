Planner 1 (for weekdays) (75 minutes)

Step 1: Gratitude (5 minutes)

Close your eyes and thank god for another day and for every little thing that you are grateful for. If there is something disturbing you, let it go becaue that does not serve you anymore. Express gratitude for your sight, body, family, anything that you can think of. Just focus on positive.

Step 2: Mindful breathing(5 minutes)

Do mindful breathing. Involve yourself and observe the pattern.

Step 3: Relax (15 minutes)

Observe your breathe. Don't try to control it. Just watch the thoughts coming in you mind and let them go. Donot get involved in any of the thought.

Step 4: Visualisation (10 minutes)

Visualise yourself doing the things you want to do in your day and the type of person you want to be. Imagine people congratulating you on your success. Listen to their voice and sound of applause.

Step 5: Exercise (20 minutes)

Go for a walk outside and do a light workout or yoga. Feel the energy in your body.

Step 6: Journaling (Before going to bed) (20 minutes)

Pour your thoughts and emotion on a virtual paper. Write every experience and emotion you faced that day. Release your worries and ease your soul.

YOU ARE READY NOW!!

HAVE A WONDERFUL DAY!!!! ROCK IT!!!
